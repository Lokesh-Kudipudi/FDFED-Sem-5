# Project Walkthrough Video Script

**Total Duration:** Approx. 8 Minutes
**Goal:** Showcase User Flows through the UI (Traveler, Hotel Manager, Tour Guide, Admin).

---

## 0:00 - 0:45 | Introduction & Home Page
**Visuals:**
- Start at **Home Page** (`/`).
- Smooth scroll down to "Discover" section.
- Hover over "Hotels" and "Tours" cards.
- Highlight the "Contact Us" or Footer area briefly.

**Audio Transcript:**
"Welcome to the comprehensive Hotel and Tour Booking Project, Chasing Horizons. This platform allows users to seamlessly book their dream stays and adventure tours, while providing robust tools for hotel managers and tour guides to manage their businesses. On the landing page, you can instantly see our top-rated services. Whether you are looking for a luxury suite or a mountain trekking experience, everything is just a click away."

---

## 0:45 - 2:30 | User Registration & Search
**Visuals:**
- Click **Sign Up**.
- Show the **Sign Up** form. (Simulate filling it out or mention it).
- Redirect to **Sign In** and log in as a User.
- Navigate to **"Hotels"** (`/hotels`).
- Type "Luxury" in the search bar.
- Apply a filter (e.g., Price or Star Rating).
- Click on a specific Hotel card to view details.

**Audio Transcript:**
"Getting started is easy. New users can create an account in seconds. Once logged in, travelers gain access to our extensive catalog. Let's look for a place to stay. By navigating to the Hotels page, users can browse all available properties. Our advanced search and filtering options allow you to narrow down results by price, amenities, or location, ensuring you find the perfect match for your needs."

---

## 2:30 - 3:30 | Hotel Booking Flow
**Visuals:**
- On **Hotel Detail Page** (`/hotels/hotel/:id`).
- Scroll through images, amenities, and reviews.
- Select Check-in and Check-out dates.
- Click **"Book Now"**.
- Show Success/Confirmation message or Payment mock step.

**Audio Transcript:**
"Detailed information is available for every hotel, including high-quality images, amenity lists, and guest reviews. Booking is straightforward: simply select your travel dates and check availability. With a single click, you can secure your reservation. The system handles the booking instantly, updating the room inventory in real-time."

---

## 3:30 - 5:00 | Tours & Customization
**Visuals:**
- Navigate to **"Tours"** (`/tours`).
- Click on a Tour Package.
- Show **Tour Detail Page** (Itinerary, Price).
- Click **"Book Tour"**.
- Navigate to **"Customize Tour"** (`/customize-tour`).
- Fill a quick form for a custom request.

**Audio Transcript:**
"For those seeking adventure, our Tours section features curated packages. You can explore daily itineraries, inclusions, and pricing. If our pre-packaged tours don't fit your schedule, use the 'Customize Tour' feature. Here, you can submit specific requirements, and our tour guides will craft a personalized experience just for you."

---

## 5:00 - 5:45 | User Dashboard
**Visuals:**
- Navigate to **User Dashboard** (`/user/dashboard`).
- Click **"My Bookings"** tab.
- Show list of Active/Past bookings.
- Click **"My Custom Requests"**.

**Audio Transcript:**
"The User Dashboard is the central hub for travelers. Here, you can view the status of all your bookings, access booking vouchers, and review your history. You can also track the status of any custom tour requests you've successfully submitted."

---

## 5:45 - 6:45 | Hotel Manager & Tour Guide Portals
**Visuals:**
- **Log Out**.
- **Log In** as **Hotel Manager**.
- Show **Manager Dashboard** (`/hotel-manager/dashboard`).
- Navigate to **"Room Inventory"**. Show adding/editing a room.
- Navigate to **"Bookings"** (`/hotel-manager/bookings`). Show checking in a guest/Confirming.

**Audio Transcript:**
"Now, let's switch perspectives. Hotel Managers have their own dedicated portal. Upon logging in, they see a dashboard summarizing occupancy and revenue. The 'Room Inventory' section allows managers to easily add new rooms or update pricing. Viewing and managing incoming bookings is efficient, allowing for smooth check-in and check-out operations."

---

## 6:45 - 7:30 | Admin Overview
**Visuals:**
- **Log Out**.
- **Log In** as **Admin**.
- Show **Admin Dashboard**.
- Briefly click on **"Manage Users"** or **"Manage Bookings"** to show oversight capabilities.

**Audio Transcript:**
"Finally, Administrators keep the platform running smoothly. The Admin Dashboard provides a bird's-eye view of the entire system. Admins can manage users, approve new hotel managers and tour guides, and oversee all bookings and financial transactions, ensuring safety and quality across the platform."

---

## 7:30 - 8:00 | Conclusion
**Visuals:**
- Return to **Home Page**.
- Show a final "Call to Action" or Logo.

**Audio Transcript:**
"Our Hotel and Tour Management System bridges the gap between travelers and service providers, creating a seamless, efficient, and enjoyable experience for everyone. Thank you for watching."
